Overview of Layered Security

Device security provides users with multiple methods to ensure the safety of solid-state drives, mitigate the risk of network viruses, and detect any unauthorized tampering or hacking of the firmware.

With the layered security approach for MFPs, customers are offered the flexibility to tailor their device security according to their specific needs. This approach empowers customers to customize the level of security measures in place, whether it be to defend against network breaches, system hacks, unauthorized access, or inadvertent data exposure (e.g., leaving sensitive documents unattended in the output tray).

The first security layer offered is the bizhub SECURE portfolio. Numerous clients are unaware that configuring the bizhub MFP is essential to mitigate vulnerabilities. Without proper configuration, there is a risk of unauthorized access to data at rest or potential infiltration of the internal network through the MFP.

The second layer that adds onto the bizhub SECURE portfolio is the Konica Minolta developed and embedded device-based security monitoring MarketPlace based APP called bizhub SECURE Notifier. This app makes it easy for our clients to check the status of their bizhub SECURE settings, including: · Administrator password · HDD/SSD encryption · HDD/SSD password protection · Auto document deletion · Temporary data overwrite (not applicable to i-Series SSD).

The bizhub SECURE Notifier app visually indicates whether or not the MFP is secure, lists the status of the security settings and allows print out status reports. Users can also be notified via email that their security settings have been compromised.

Building upon the foundation of bizhub SECURE and bizhub SECURE Notifier, we have further enhanced protection with the introduction of an added layer of protection with real-time Antivirus and Malware protection. By partnering with Bitdefender©, a worldwide leader in cybersecurity, a fully functional and feature-rich, on-board Antivirus and Malware solution for i-Series MFPs.

Part 1: How you can provide value

In healthcare, the handling of sensitive patient data such as medical records, prescriptions, and billing information is routine. Any breach or mishandling of this data can lead to severe consequences—both in terms of fines and loss of patient trust. Many healthcare providers may not realize that printers, scanners, and copiers can be vulnerable points in their data security strategy.

Konica Minolta's Layered Security Approach:

Our MFPs offer a multi-layered approach to security, specifically designed to meet healthcare industry standards and regulations. Here is how Konica Minolta can help healthcare providers safeguard their practice and patient data across all phases-whether it's being scanned, printed, or stored.

Key Security Features:

  1. HIPAA Compliance: bizhub MFPs are built with features that ensure compliance with HIPAA requirements. They provide comprehensive access controls, encryption, and secure document handling to protect patient health information (PHI). This makes it easier for our clients to meet regulatory standards while focusing on patient care.
  2. Data Encryption and Overwrite Protection: All sensitive data, including scanned patient records or printed prescriptions, is encrypted during processing and storage. In addition, bizhub MFPs include automatic data overwrite features that erase all documents that may be stored on the device after a specified time, making it impossible for unauthorized users to recover patient information.
  3. User Authentication for Access Control: To prevent unauthorized access to patient records and other sensitive documents, bizhub MFPs offer user authentication options, such as PIN codes, ID badges, or biometric scans. This ensures that only authorized healthcare staff can print, scan, or access sensitive documents. User authentication is crucial in busy healthcare settings where shared devices are common.
  4. Secure Print Release: In a fast-paced healthcare environment, sensitive documents can easily be left on a MFP output tray, exposing patient data to unauthorized personnel. Our Secure Print Release feature ensures that documents are only printed when the authorized staff member is physically at the device. Secure Print means that the authorized user must authenticate to release the printed document. This reduces the risk of documents sitting uncollected and unsecured on the output tray.
  5. Storage Encryption and Security: bizhub MFPs use full-disk encryption to ensure that any data stored on the device's solid state drive is unreadable without proper access. Additionally, we offer storage drive locking capabilities that prevent removal or tampering, adding an extra layer of security.
  6. Automatic Threat Detection and Response: Healthcare data is a prime target for cyberattacks. With our Layered Security approach, bizhub MFPs are hardened prior to installation in the client's network. This means we can lock down the system, and alert the client to any suspicious activity. Automatic threat detection and response can significantly reduce the risk of data breaches.

Enhanced Compliance Features:

Not only do our bizhub MFPs protect patient data, but they also support audit trails and detailed logging of user activity. This means our clients have complete visibility into who accessed or printed sensitive information. This is key for both internal monitoring and regulatory audits, ensuring that healthcare providers remain compliant with HIPAA, HITECH, and other healthcare regulations.

Main Benefits For the Customers

  • Confidentiality: Healthcare providers can have confidence that their sensitive documents and data are secure, reducing the risk of unauthorized access or data breaches.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Implementing robust data security measures helps customers meet compliance requirements mandated by regulations such as HIPAA, Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX) and PCI-DSS, GDPR avoiding potential fines or penalties for non-compliance.
  • Protection of Patient Health Information: Healthcare providers can safeguard their PHI from theft or unauthorized disclosure, preserving their reputations, and business integrity.
  • Increased Trust and Reputation: Demonstrating a commitment to data security instills trust and confidence among patients, employees, and the larger community, enhancing the Healthcare provider's reputation and credibility in the marketplace.
  • Cost Savings: Proactively addressing data security concerns can help Healthcare providers avoid the financial costs associated with data breaches, such as legal fees, and regulatory fines.
  • Improved Productivity: Secure printing practices and streamlined document workflows enable customers to enhance productivity by minimizing the risk of document loss, ensuring timely access to critical information, and reducing manual intervention in document handling processes.
  • Simplified Compliance Audits: By implementing robust data security measures on MFPs, Healthcare providers can streamline compliance audits and reporting processes, saving time and resources required for demonstrating adherence to regulatory requirements.
