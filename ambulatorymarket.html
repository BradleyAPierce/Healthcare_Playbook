What is the
Ambulatory Care Setting?

Ambulatory Care providers deliver health services offered on an outpatient basis without admission in a hospital for treatment. Physician practices and urgent care centers are the most common sites of Ambulatory Care. Other Ambulatory Care providers include same–day surgical centers, dental practices, vision practices, and diagnostic and imaging centers that offer blood tests, X–rays, and similar services. Ambulatory Care can also be delivered in health centers in universities, schools, and prisons, and in pharmacies.
