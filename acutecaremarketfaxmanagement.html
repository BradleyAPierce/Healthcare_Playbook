Fax Technology Overview

Direct Connectivity

No document can be transmitted unless the outbound fax device has a direct connection with the receiving device.

Cloud Fax

  • Uses Internet to send and receive
  • Epic EHR integration using Paragon Cloud Fax
  • Transmits faxed documents as secure attachments
  • Encrypts messages before transmission
  • Reduces local infrastructure maintenance
  • Eliminates paper–based workflow

Fax Server

  • Installed in a local area network
  • Automates delivery, receipt, and tracking of transmitted documents
  • Eliminates error prone and time consuming manual work
  • Reduces or eliminates cost of paper and toner
  • Eliminates stand–alone fax devices and their maintenance
  • Simplifies file storage

Qualifying Questions

Use this information when you: 1. Prepare for your appointments 2. Prepare your Value Statements 3. Choose Konica Minolta and All Covered content to share with prospects.

Qualifying Questions
Qualifying Questions

    What are the current challenges facing the firm with regard to fax?

    What types of documents are you sending and receiving?

    Ask if fax is used to send and receive any of the following document types: Patient health records, finance and accounting documents, contracts, purchase orders, and human resources documents

    What concerns do you have about compliance risks in your current fax deployment?

    What is your fax volume on a weekly or monthly basis? How many documents do you send by fax? How much fax traffic do you receive?

    What is your forecast for growth or decline in your fax volume?

    How much are you currently spending on fax?

    What fax devices are currently in use? Ask about fax servers, dedicated fax machines, and fax–enabled MFPs.

    How much does your staff need to retransmit faxes?

    How much would your organization benefit from eliminating stand–alone fax devices?

    What concerns do you have about the time required to perform manual paper–based faxing?

    How would you benefit from having an audit trail for all fax documents sent and received?

    What applications do you intend to use for sending and receiving? Ask about intended use of email, MFP solutions, or other applications.

    What is your current E H R system?

    What is your current telephone infrastructure? Ask about VoIP, SIP, Digital, Analog. If your current fax devices use analog telephony, are there plans for fax over IP?
