Become a Healthcare Certified Professional!

Enhance your skills and advance your sales potential in healthcare.

Earning your Healthcare Certification is a key step toward becoming a trusted advisor in one of the most complex and rewarding industries we serve. These nine courses are designed to give you a comprehensive understanding of healthcare systems, regulations, workflows, and the unique challenges your customers face. As you progress through each module, you'll gain the insight needed to position Konica Minolta's solutions with confidence and credibility. This certification will help you identify and engage more qualified healthcare prospects, uncover new opportunities, and align solutions to their specific care environments. By mastering these topics, you'll expand your expertise, accelerate your sales success, and strengthen your role as a valued healthcare partner.

Healthcare Certification Program Acknowledgment

Direct Channel

The healthcare certification program has been developed to provide a level of healthcare industry competency for members of our sales organization who are dedicated to, or have a highly focused concentration on this critical vertical market. The coursework included in the certification program provides a foundational understanding of the healthcare industry and the provider organizations categorized as Acute, Ambulatory, and Extended Care, as well as the trends, challenges, initiatives, and the solutions and services developed and positioned to address this critical vertical market.

The certification program includes the following steps, as described below. By downloading and acknowledging this document, you agree to pursue and complete these steps prior to finalizing the certification process.

Required:
  • Complete assigned certification curriculum.
  • Access and utilize the Healthcare Digital Playbook.
  • Join the monthly Healthcare Webinar.
  • Film a brief presentation via Teams, and submit files to the following:
    jcisna@kmbs.konicaminolta.us
    MCardwell@kmbs.konicaminolta.us
    dmorrison@allcovered.com
Recommended:
  • Follow Healthcare Social Media Targets.
  • Download internal and external Healthcare Battle Cards.
  • Obtain prescribed digital marketing collateral.
  • Join local HIMSS Chapter, MGMA, or other membership organizations in your market (as appropriate).
