(GPO) Group Purchasing Organizations

Group Purchasing Organizations are a significant player in the healthcare industry.

  1. Healthcare Playbook
  2. Sales Practices
  3. (GPO) Group Purchasing Organizations

What are
Group Purchasing Organizations?

  • GPOs provide purchasing power to healthcare organizations of various sizes, from a single physician to a vast healthcare network.
  • GPOs have contracts with many kinds of suppliers, including Konica Minolta.
  • GPOs can help a healthcare organization purchase medical supplies, business equipment, and professional services.
