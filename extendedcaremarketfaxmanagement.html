Fax Technology Overview

Direct Connectivity

No document can be transmitted unless the outbound fax device has a direct connection with the receiving device.

Cloud Fax

  • Uses Internet to send and receive faxes
  • EHR integration via the Kno2 Cloud Fax Platform with existing integrations into PointClickCare, Yardi, MatrixCare SygmaCare and LPC EHRs, and other popular systems
  • Transmits faxed documents as secure attachments
  • Encrypts messages before transmission
  • Reduces local infrastructure maintenance
  • Eliminates paper–based workflow

Fax Server

  • Installed in a local area network
  • Automates delivery, receipt, and tracking of transmitted documents
  • Eliminates error prone and time consuming manual work
  • Reduces or eliminates cost of paper and toner
  • Eliminates stand–alone fax devices and their maintenance
  • Simplifies file storage

Benefits, Pain Points and Technology Trends
in Healthcare Fax Management

  • Care transitions
  • Disparate electronic health record systems (EHR) across the care continuum – reliance on fax
  • Legacy communications – especially fax machines – can create several challenges:
    • Missing pages & garbled/unreadable images
    • Time consuming processing to intake documentation
    • Attaching to the patient record in a usable and meaningful way
    • POTS lines becoming expensive
    • HIPAA breach risk when PHI left unattended in output tray

Qualifying Questions

Use this information when you: 1. Prepare for your appointments 2. Prepare your Value Statements 3. Choose Konica Minolta and All Covered content to share with prospects.

Qualifying Questions
Qualifying Questions
  1. Which EHR are you using?
  2. What types of documents are you sending and receiving?
  3. What concerns do you have about compliance risks in your current fax deployment?
  4. What is your fax volume on a weekly or monthly basis? How many documents do you send by fax? How much fax traffic do you receive?
  5. What is your forecast for growth or decline in your fax volume?
  6. How much are you currently spending on fax?
  7. What fax devices are currently in use? Ask about fax servers, dedicated fax machines, and fax–enabled MFPs.
  8. How would you benefit from having an audit trail for all fax documents sent and received?
  9. What applications do you intend to use for sending and receiving? Ask about intended use of email, MFP solutions, or other applications.
