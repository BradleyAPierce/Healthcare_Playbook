Pain Points of Traditional Fax in Healthcare
Back to Home Screen
Traditional fax devices and fax lines - although HIPAA compliant - pose serious security risks to healthcare organizations. These  risks include unauthorized access to Protected Health Information, or PHI, and financial penalties for non-compliance with HIPAA. These risks are driving more attention to cloud fax services in all healthcare settings. By replacing traditional fax with cloud fax, healthcare leaders can mitigate the risks associated with physical fax infrastructure.
