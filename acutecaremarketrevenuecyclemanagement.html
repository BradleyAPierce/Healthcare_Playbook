How You Can Add Value
Listen for the following during calls and appointments:
  • Our staff has to track down information that we are not capturing during patient registration
  • We‛re not always verifying insurance information properly
  • Our process for authorizing treatment takes too much time
  • Our process for pre–certification with the payers takes too much time
  • We are experiencing more claims denials, and the work to manage them is a burden
  • We do not receive the level of reimbursement we expect on our claims
  • We need to recover underpayment on our submitted claims
  • We are concerned about the accuracy of our coding
  • Other providers send us referrals for patient who don‛t make appointments
  • We are receiving referrals with incomplete information from the referring provider organization
  • Credentialing our clinical staff is a big administrative burden
  • We have a backlog in Accounts Receivable
  • We need to improve our Accounts Receivable analytics

Qualifying Questions

Use this information when you: 1. Prepare for your appointments 2. Prepare your Value Statements 3. Choose Konica Minolta and All Covered content to share with prospects.

Qualifying Questions
Qualifying Questions
  1. How are you currently capturing patient registration information?
  2. Are you using pre–printed forms or digital forms? Can you automate the capture of information from the forms?
  3. What types of patient information do you enter manually into your EHR?
  4. How does your patient intake staff verify insurance information?
  5. How often do you contact referring providers to obtain missing information?
  6. Would you consider automating your referral process? How would automating your referral process impact your organization?
  7. How does your staff manage patient scheduling?
  8. Do you provide any estimation of costs or financial counseling to patients? How do you provide that service?
  9. Is your medical coding and claims submission workflow able to keep up with the workload?
  10. What is the impact of claims denials on your organization?
  11. How are you currently managing claims denials?
  12. Are you tracking underpayment on claims? If so, how are you managing the recovery of revenue from underpayment?
  13. Do you need to improve collections from overdue accounts? How are you managing your collections workflow?
  14. How are you measuring performance in Accounts Receivable? Would you benefit from more robust analysis of time in A/R?
