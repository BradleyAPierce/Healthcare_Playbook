How Healthcare Cloud Meets Healthcare Provider Challenges

  1. Security and Reliability: Our Healthcare Cloud provides fully–managed virtual servers housed in our secure data centers. Our Healthcare Cloud has passed the most stringent audits available. We offer a 99.995% uptime guarantee.
  2. Capital spending to maintain IT infrastructure: The Healthcare Cloud is Infrastructure as a Service, or IaaS. This means the customer pays a monthly operating expense, not a capital expense. This is important for growing healthcare organizations that struggle to mobilize the capital spending needed to grow and maintain their IT infrastructure.
  3. System maintenance and upgrades: When using the Healthcare Cloud, the customer does not need to maintain the system, or purchase software and hardware to perform upgrades.
  4. Rapid scalability: When healthcare organizations grow rapidly, they can be confident that our services will be able to rapidly scale up with them. When a customer needs a new Server, we can quickly deploy one that‛s customized to meet our customer‛s specific needs.
  5. Availability of Software Applications on Any Device: Our Healthcare Cloud allows our customers to plug in and connect via the Internet to applications on our secure cloud infrastructure.

Overcoming Objections

Objection 1: "I have no time– I‛m too busy now."

Response

Thanks for sharing your concern. I know your time is valuable. In only 20 minutes, I can share information about how we _____ I believe you will find this to be time well spent. Which day and time will work for you?

Objection 2: "I have a vendor – I‛m all set... "

Response 1

Thanks for sharing that with me. How is your vendor helping you assess your current needs and growth in IT infrastructure? I can offer you valuable information about our expertise in assessing your current state and comparing it to your strategy for your future. Which day and time will work for you to learn more?

Response 2

Thanks, I understand. Quick question– When was the last time a vendor provided an accurate assessment of your current IT infrastructure so that you could make data–driven improvements in technology environment? I am offering you a valuable opportunity to spend a relatively small amount of time that could lead to big gains for you.

Thanks, I understand. Quick question– Are you completely satisfied with your current vendor‛s performance? I assure you that your decision to meet with me briefly will pay off for you. Which day and time next week works best for you?

Qualifying Questions

Use this information when you: 1. Prepare for your appointments 2. Prepare your Value Statements 3. Choose Konica Minolta and All Covered content to share with prospects.

Qualifying Questions
Qualifying Questions
  1. How well will your existing IT infrastructure handle your needs for the next 1–3 years? If you are not confident that current infrastructure will handle your needs for the near future, what is your current plan to address it?
  2. Is having instant access to your data from anywhere and on any device important to you?
  3. How accessible is your existing IT infrastructure for your employees working out of the office?
  4. Is system reliability an issue? – Is your hardware the cause of your reliability issues?
  5. How essential is guaranteed system uptime for your business?
  6. How prepared are you and your staff to respond to a sudden network outage or unplanned downtime?
  7. Can you describe your backup plan for downtime?
  8. Can your company absorb the losses from extended network downtime?
  9. Would you like to know how you can minimize downtime even in such worst–case scenarios?
  10. What procedures do you have in place to retrieve lost data in case of server failure?
  11. How safe is your data in case of a sudden network outage?
  12. Are you thinking about whether cloud services would be a useful part of your IT strategy?
  13. Are you planning a move – and want your system to be accessible even while you‛re relocating your office space?
  14. Would you like to be able to save on hardware and software costs and not have to worry about upgrades anymore?
