Value Based Care Overview

What is Value Based Care?



Rollover to reveal information

What is Value Based Care?

Value Based Care is a healthcare delivery model in which hospitals, physicians, and other providers receive payment based on patient outcomes.

What is the Value?



Rollover to reveal information

What is the Value?

The value in Value Based Care is evidence from measuring the patients‛ health outcomes against the costs of delivering those outcomes.

How are hospitals and physicians paid in a Value Based Care model?



Rollover to reveal information

How are hospitals and physicians paid in a Value Based Care model?

In Value Based Care, providers receive payment for helping patients improve their health or recover from disease. Evidence must show that patients have better health as a result of the providers‛ care.

What makes Value Based Care different?



Rollover to reveal information

What makes Value Based Care different?

Value Based Care differs from the traditional fee–for–service model. In fee–for–service healthcare, hospitals and physicians receive payment based on the amount of services they perform for the patients, regardless of the patient outcomes.

How does Value Based Care affect the Healthcare Market?



Rollover to reveal information

How does Value Based Care affect the Healthcare Market?

Value Based Care requires a transition from the fee–for–service model to payment based on patient health outcomes. In this transition, hospitals and physicians must adopt standards of care that are based on evidence of improving patient health. Healthcare insurers must be able to process information that demonstrates improvement in patient outcomes.

Qualifying Questions

  2. How are you attaining your goals for Value Based Care?
  3. What steps are you currently taking to improve patient outcomes?
  4. How are you using your clinical data to improve patient outcomes?
  5. How are you quickly consolidating and sharing your clinical data?
  6. What are your current efforts to improve patient satisfaction?
  7. What specific patient satisfaction challenges are you trying to solve?
  8. How are you using predictive analytics to deliver care?
  9. How would a service that analyzes your clinical data benefit you and your organization?

  14. How would a service that can assist with Population Health Management benefit you and your organization?
