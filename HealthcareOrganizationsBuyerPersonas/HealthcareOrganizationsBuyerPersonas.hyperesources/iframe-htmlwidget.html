Description

The CIO oversees the people, processes and technologies within a company‛s IT organization to ensure they deliver outcomes that support the goals of the business. The CIO is responsible for the roadmap for information technology throughout the organization.

Responsibilities

Provide leadership for all information technology personnel and practices. Provide strategic planning, development, evaluation and coordination of all information technology systems.

Top Concerns

Information technology costs and investments. Cybersecurity and threat mitigation. Performance of all IT systems. Mobility, Mergers and acquisitions that require integration of disparate systems. Interoperability of systems and applications. Strategic planning and digital transformation. Hiring and retaining IT talent. Outsourcing of services.
