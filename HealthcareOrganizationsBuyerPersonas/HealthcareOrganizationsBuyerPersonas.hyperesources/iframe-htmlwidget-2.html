Description

A CMIO leads the analysis of health information and data to improve patient outcomes. A CMIO serves must collaborate with the medical and information technology departments to ensure that patient health data is readily accessible to clinical staff while also protecting protecting that data and ensuring compliance with HIPAA.

Responsibilities

Evaluate the IT Systems, especially the HER software and applications, to improve patient care. Analyze health data to improve clinical services and daily patient care. Train physicians and other clinical staff in effective use of IT systems, especially EHR and digital physician order entry. Ensure quality of care across multiple locations.

Top Concerns

Efficiency in use of the Electronic Health Records system, especially data entry. Use of data to improve health outcomes for all patients. Ability to monitor health outcomes of specific patient populations through data analysis.Interoperability of different health applications and E H R systems. Documenting and reporting meaningful use of E H R technology to comply with federal mandates.
