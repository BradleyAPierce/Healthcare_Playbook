Description

A Practice Manager supervises business and administrative aspects of a medical practice, diagnostic center, or ambulatory surgery center, where staff includes physicians, nurses, medical assistants, laboratory specialists, imaging specialists, and office personnel.

Responsibilities

A Practice Manager directs administrative and business operations in ambulatory care. This role can include supervision of patient records and services, billing, payroll, staff performance, and hiring of support staff.

Top Concerns

  • Maintain compliance with HIPAA and other regulations
  • Providing clinical staff access to patient health information
  • Training staff who perform tasks such as scheduling and coding
  • Managing patient satisfaction
  • Fluctuations in recurring appointments
  • Managing staff turnover, Managing the burden of paperwork in patient records.
