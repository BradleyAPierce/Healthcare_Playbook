Description

A President or CEO leads all aspects of the business, especially the strategy to ensure financial stability and growth of the business.

Responsibilities

Strategic planning; Planning budgets, Managing the revenue and costs to provide a stable financial environment; Leading the supervisors within the facility to help meet business goals.

Top Concerns

  • Declining census rates (need to increase outreach efforts with referral partners to drive more inbound referral business)
  • High staff turnover
  • Regulatory compliance (HIPAA, COVID, new reimbursement requirements such PDPM for Skilled Nursing Facilities, etc)
  • Taking higher acuity patients increases the demands on skilled nursing facilities. It would be easy to turn these patients down, but in order to remain competitive, facilities are accepting these challenging cases
