Description

An Administrator leads and supervises all day–to–day operations involving the organization, resident care, and business processes.

Responsibilities

Support the CEO in implementing the strategy; Supervise, plan, develop, monitor and maintain appropriate standards of resident care throughout all departments; Hire personnel and provide necessary training.

Top Concerns

  • Managing staff to ensure that they perform duties in compliance with all the legal and regulatory requirements
  • Threats to financial stability due to shortfalls in Medicaid funding
  • Limited budgets to support daily operations
  • Risk of net financial losses
  • Financial problems due to the cost to fight the COVID pandemic.
