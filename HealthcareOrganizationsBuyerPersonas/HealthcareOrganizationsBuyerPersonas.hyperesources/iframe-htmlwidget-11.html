Description

A Compliance Manager develops and implements the processes to maintain compliance with healthcare regulations.

Responsibilities

HIPAA compliance and protection of patient health information; Establishes guidelines for all staff to follow.

Top Concerns

  • Unauthorized access to patient records
  • Theft or exposure of PHI via external or internal breaches of security, Prevention of HIPAA violations and resulting fines
  • Ensuring confidentiality of patient information during transfer of records to and from other healthcare providers.
