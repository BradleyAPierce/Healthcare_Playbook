Description

An Information Technology Director or Manager supervises the technology infrastructure of the ambulatory group, including network operations and network security.

Responsibilities

Manages implementation and ongoing maintenance of IT infrastructure including all network hardware, networked devices used by personnel, and software applications used for clinical and business operations.

Top Concerns

  • Choosing right technology for clinical work, security of PHI, and business operations
  • Preventing unauthorized access to the network, Ensuring interoperability of applications and other technology assets, Maintaining data integrity, Updating hardware and software to support the operation and functions of the ambulatory group
  • Providing staff training, when needed, for effective use of new technology.
