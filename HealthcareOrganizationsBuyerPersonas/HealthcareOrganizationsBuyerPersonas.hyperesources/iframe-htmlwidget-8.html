Description

A Health Information Manager organizes and protects all patient health information.

Responsibilities

Ensuring that clinical staff can access accurate and complete health information when and where they need it; ensuring that patient information is accurately entered into the electronic health records system.

Top Concerns

  • Managing increases in the volume of PHI to be handled and processed by staff
  • Managing complexity in an integrated patient record in the E H R system
  • Ensuring that PHI is viewed and processed by authorized staff.
