Description

A Facility Director leads the management of the building and the services necessary to support and maintain the building and its equipment.

Responsibilities

Supervising and hiring workers, making staff schedules, supervising building maintenance activities, and fostering collaboration among the staff and with vendors who provide services to the facility.

Top Concerns

  • Revenue stream to support the facility
  • Inadequate Medicaid reimbursement, declining private–pay population numbers, CMS penalties for readmissions
