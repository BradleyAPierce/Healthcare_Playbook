Description

The CFO is the senior leader that manages all financial functions. As the financial leader, the CFO plays a vital role in planning and carrying out business strategy.

Responsibilities

Provide leadership for all finance and accounting personnel and formation of financial policies. Responsible for managing assets and liabilities, controlling margins and debt, and managing cash flow to ensure financial stability. Also responsible for all financial and budget planning, financial reporting, and analysis of financial strengths and weaknesses.

Top Concerns

Revenue cycle management, declining reimbursements, managing the impact of increasing costs, streamlining financial operations to improve productivity and efficiency, increased reliance on IT, staff turnover, non compliance with regulations.
