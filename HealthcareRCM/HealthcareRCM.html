Complete Patient Registration It is essential to create an error–free patient account with complete health history and insurance coverages.
Capture Services and Charges Healthcare providers must thoroughly and accurately capture charges for delivery of patient care.
Coding Complete and accurate coding to create a healthcare claim is essential to avoid delays.
Duration for Claims ProcessingProcessing of claims can extend over a period of months.
Claims DenialHealthcare organizations must manage denial of claims for reasons such as improper coding, or information missing from the patient health record. One third of hospital executives reported that payers denied more than 10 percent of claims submitted, on average. (Based on a 2021 survey of 131 hospital leaders reported by consulting firm Harmony Healthcare)
Value Based CareWith value based care payment models, hospitals and medical practices must document and report measures of quality health outcomes and patient satisfaction.
CollectionsFor any charges not covered by insurance claims, healthcare organizations must collect payments from patients.
Avoiding Bad DebtAcute and Ambulatory providers must estimate the costs of care and embrace transparency by informing patients early of their payment responsibility.
Referral “Leakage" Providers want to ensure that patients referred to them get scheduled for appointments. Every incoming referral represents a potential source of revenue.
