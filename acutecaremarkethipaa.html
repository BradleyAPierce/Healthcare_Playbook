How You Can Provide Value
HIPAA Compliance Services from All Covered

We provide value by conducting a thorough and ongoing risk assessment to measure compliance with HIPAA and HITECH regulations.

Our assessment service can show clients the vulnerabilities that put their electronic protected health information, or ePHI, at risk. Your message to acute care organizations is that Risk Assessment should be an ongoing process.


How often should they assess risk?

Acute care organizations and their third party suppliers should conduct their risk assessments annually. They should revisit their analysis any time there is a change in the operating or technical environment.

How our HIPAA expertise adds value

We evaluate the effectiveness of security measures in place

  • We track access to ePHI to verify that access aligns with regulations and policy
  • We detect security incidents that put ePHI at risk
  • We can provide guidance on HIPAA policy development
  • We can deliver security awareness training for employees
  • We can maximize the security of documents at the MFP

Use any of the ideas above to create value statements for your prospecting phone calls and email messages. Use the Persona Worksheet.

Risk Assessment

HIPAA Compliance Services from All Covered provides value to both the acute care organization, and its business associates, by conducting a thorough and ongoing risk assessment to measure compliance with HIPAA and HITECH regulations.


Our assessment service can show the client the vulnerabilities that put their electronic protected health information at risk.


To be compliant, acute care organizations and their third party suppliers should conduct their risk assessment annually and revisit their analysis any time there is a change in the operating or technical environment.


Your message to acute care organizations is that Risk Assessment should be an ongoing process.

Overcoming Objections

Objection 1: “I have no time–I'm too busy now.”


Response

Thanks for sharing your concern about time. I know your time is valuable. In only 20 minutes, I can share information about how we assure that our customers comply with all HIPAA regulations. I believe you will find this to be time well spent. Which day and time will work for you?


Objection 2: I have a vendor – I'm all set...


Response

Thanks for sharing that with me. How is your vendor helping you assess your current compliance with HIPAA? How is your vendor helping you assess any vulnerabilities that may put your patient information at risk? I can offer you our vast experience and deep expertise in services to assess and support HIPAA compliance. Which day and time will work for you to learn more?
