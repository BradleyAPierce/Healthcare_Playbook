Better patient safety and outcomes

A clinical professional can rapidly access patient records from different health systems. If someone has a health emergency away from the primary care practice or hometown hospital, a local clinical worker can obtain a medical history.

Improved Timeliness

Rapid sharing of PHI across systems can replace time-consuming manual efforts to exchange patient records and prescriptions. Clinical staff caring for a patient transferred from one system to another need not wait for the health records to be exchanged.

Improved Quality and Accuracy

Digital document exchange means eliminating misplaced paper health records. This ensures receipt of the complete health record and better clinical decision making to help the patient.

Ensure greater security and compliance with HIPAA

Ensure greater security and compliance with HIPAA: The health records are kept secure in the exchange. This prevents damaging and costly violations of HIPAA.

Qualifying Questions

Use this information when you: 1. Prepare for your appointments 2. Prepare your Value Statements 3. Choose Konica Minolta and All Covered content to share with prospects.

Qualifying Questions
Qualifying Questions
  1. Do you regularly send and receive patient health information?
  2. Do you use fax as the main way to exchange patient information in your organization?
  3. Have you considered moving away from fax as a primary tool for patient information exchange?
  4. Is your PHI all electronic or do you rely on paper-based PHI?
  5. Are you looking for ways to streamline your inbound order and referral process?
  6. Have you considered using Direct Secure Messaging for exchange of patient health information?

    7. The following are questions to ask when you know your contact already has an EHR system:


  7. Does your organization exchange PHI with other practices or facilities that lack an EHR system?
  8. Does your organization manage or own any practices or facilities that use different EHR systems? If so, how are you exchanging information today?
  9. What is your strategy for integrating separate systems?
  10. Do you exchange electronic health records with other facilities that use only fax as a means of exchanging patient health information?
  11. Are you attempting to close any gaps in connectivity between your facility / your practice and affiliated facilities / practices in the community?
  12. Does your EHR system support Direct Secure Messaging?
  13. Do you want to improve your community referral partners ability to electronically communicate with your organization?
  14. Would you consider connecting your systems with Health Level 7 interfaces for exchanging patient health information electronically?
  15. Are you acquiring or merging with healthcare systems that need HL7 interfaces in order to support exchange of electronic patient health information?
  16. Do you need expert help to support and monitor HL7 interfaces in your system?
