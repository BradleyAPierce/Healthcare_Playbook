Why Managed Print Services Matter

Regulatory, economic and public health crises are making the business of healthcare more challenging than ever, and the need to manage operational expenses is critical. As healthcare organizations pursue digital transformation strategies, streamlining otherwise costly and legacy processes, such as the print environment can be equally crucial.

One of the last bastions of unaudited expense and potential Protected Health Information (PHI) exposure exists within the print environment. Although nearly 100% of hospitals and health systems have adopted and implemented electronic health records (EHRs) over the past 10 years, the use of paper continues to be prevalent.

How You Can Provide Value
Your value message for Managed Print Services in Healthcare

MPS in Healthcare – Konica Minolta‛s Managed Print Services for the Healthcare enterprise provides overall fleet management of your networked and local printers, scanners, label printers and other similar peripherals.

Assessment Process
Assessment options: Describe our assessment process.

Konica Minolta conducts a thorough assessment of the clinic‛s current print environment (both clinical and administrative), EHR requirements and needs, capture networked device output and identify usage patterns and print volumes. The data is used to design an optimized placement and document production environment to reduce costs, enhance workflows and maximize uptime, which is critical when patient care is at stake.


  • ASSESSMENT LAUNCH – Fleet Monitoring combined with a physical site survey and site mapping software identifies business processes, workflow challenges and how information moves through your healthcare organization.
  • COST COLLECTION – Costs associated with print, processes and how you manage and move information in your healthcare organization and across your network of care providers are gathered.
  • INFORMATION SHARING – Assessment results, data collected, processes and all costs are reviewed.
  • SOLUTIONS AND RECOMMENDATIONS – Our team of experts recommend potential areas for:
    • Savings
    • Business Process Streamlining
    • Improved Efficiency
    • Waste Reduction
    • Process Error Reduction

Qualifying Questions

Use this information when you: 1. Prepare for your appointments 2. Prepare your Value Statements 3. Choose Konica Minolta and All Covered content to share with prospects.

Qualifying Questions
Qualifying Questions
  1. How are you currently managing your printers and multifunction devices?
  2. Do you know what you spend on print each month? per year?
  3. How important is it to reduce the costs of your print environment?
  4. How much of your IT staff time is spent responding to printer issues?
  5. How much printer down time do you experience because you run out of supplies or experience breakdowns?
  6. How do you know if your printers and MFDs are overused or under–used?
  7. Are there any specific print challenges that you are trying to solve?
  8. Who typically feels the impact of these challenges?
  9. Have you quantified the impact?
  10. Are your print costs based on a monthly print volume?
  11. Are your print costs based on a count of color and black–and–white prints?
  12. Who services your print devices? How satisfied are you with the service you receive?
  13. Are your monthly invoices for print clear and easy to understand?
