Fax Technology Trends In Healthcare 
Volume TrendsFax volume is steady or increasing in most healthcare organizations. The chief reason for fax volume growth is integration with email and business systems.
Fax MethodsUse of stand-alone devices is expected to decline. Cloud fax services are expected to grow in volume as organizations phase out stand-alone fax machines.  Fax servers in local networks are not expected to grow as much as cloud fax services.
ConsolidationMost organizations are consolidating all faxing into a single solution.
IntegrationMost fax users have already integrated or are evaluating integration of fax with their business applications or other technologies. Workflow automation is one of the drivers of this trend.
